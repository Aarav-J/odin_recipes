BlueBerry Flax Pancakes

Description:

Fluffy panackes with ground flax seed and blueberries for a healthier, fiber fulled pancakes. Very quick recipe, and is only going to take you a few minutes. 4 servings.

Ingrediants:

  • 1½ cups fry pancake mix
  • ½ cups flax seed meal
  • 1 cup skim milk
  • 2 eggs
  • a cup fresh or thawed frozen blueberries

Steps:

  1. Set a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. In a medium, bowl, stir together the pancake mix and flax seed mea. In a seperate bowl or measuring cup, wihsk together the milk and eggs. Pour the liqiod into the dry ingredients, and stir just until moistened
  3. Spoon 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the hot skillet. Sprinkle with as many blueberries as desired. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.
